Our Story

A little corner of Italy
in South Tyneside.

Olivetto was born in 2023 when Ilhan, Jeanette and the team set out to bring proper Italian cooking — and proper Italian hospitality — to The Nook. Three years on, we are still doing it the way we always wanted to: real ingredients, unhurried service, and a dining room that feels like home.

Whether you are popping in for a weekday lunch, treating someone to a slow-cooked Sunday roast or sharing a bottle of red on a Friday night, you will be looked after.

On The Menu

Classics, cooked properly.

A taste of what we serve — full menu available in-house and on Just Eat. Tell us your dietary needs and we will tailor most dishes for you.

Wood-fired Pizza

Crisp bases, San Marzano tomato, fior di latte. From the Margherita to fully-loaded house specials.

Fresh Pasta

Carbonara, lasagne, ragù — the dishes regulars come back for, week after week.

Prime Steaks

Hand-cut steaks finished with our house sauces and a generous side of seasonal vegetables.

Sunday Roast

Beef, Yorkshire pudding, potato skins and proper trimmings. Sundays, 12 — 6pm.

Risottos & Burgers

Creamy risottos and hand-pressed burgers for when you fancy something a bit different.

Vegan, Veggie & GF

Vegetarian friendly with vegan and gluten-free options across the menu. Just ask the team.
