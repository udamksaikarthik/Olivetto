Our Story

A little corner of Italy
in South Tyneside.

Olivetto was born in 2023 when Sener, Habib and the team set out to bring proper Italian cooking and proper Italian hospitality to The Nook. Three years on, we are still doing it the way we always wanted to: real ingredients, unhurried service, and a dining room that feels like home.

Whether you are popping in for a weekday lunch, treating someone to a slow-cooked Sunday roast or sharing a bottle of red on a Friday night, you will be looked after.
